Project Report

due 12/14/2017 before midnight via Learning Suite 100 possible points

Format

Your report must be submitted in PDF format. The cover page should contain your team name, the names of all team members, and a nice photo (or rendering) of your aircraft. There is a page limit of 50 pages not including appendices. Refer to the BYU ME Writing Materials for general writing guidance.

At a minimum be sure to address the following in your report:

  • Design Strategies
  • Configuration Details and Sizing (with three-view drawings)
  • Airfoil and Wing Aerodynamics
  • Weights
  • Stability
  • Propulsion System
  • Overall Performance
  • Control Strategy and Testing
  • Construction Details
  • RC Flight Testing
  • Autonomous Waypoint Navigation Results (plot multiple views of full trajectories)
  • Conclusions

Grading

  • 35%: Quality of aircraft design process. Are the design choices reasonable and clearly justified? Are the analyses technically sound?
  • 30%: Quality of report. Is the writing clear and well organized? Are figures/data presented clearly? Does the information contain the important details while still being concise?
  • 20%: Quality of built aircraft. Is there evidence of good construction technique and improvements made? Is there evidence of good RC flight testing and performance?
  • 15%: Autopilot performance. How many waypoints were reached autonomously? Did the aircraft stay within boundaries and altitude limits? How long did it take to hit the waypoints? Is there evidence of a good controls testing and development process (i.e., Gazebo and ground testing)?
