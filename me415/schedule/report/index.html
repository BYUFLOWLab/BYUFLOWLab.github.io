Project Report

due 12/14/2017 before midnight via Learning Suite 100 possible points

Format

Your report must be submitted in PDF format. The cover page should contain your team name, the names of all team members, and a nice photo (or rendering) of your aircraft. There is a page limit of 50 pages not including appendices. Refer to the BYU ME Writing Materials for general writing guidance.

Rubric

Design:

  • [5 pts] overall configuration choices are soundly justified.
  • [5 pts] airfoil selection is soundly justified.
  • [5 pts] wing design is soundly justified.
  • [5 pts] predicted and measured weights are shown with discussion on how it affected the design if applicable.
  • [5 pts] tail/canard sizing is soundly justified.
  • [5 pts] a proper static and dynamic stability analysis is shown with discussion on how it affected the design.
  • [5 pts] an analysis of the propulsion system is included.
  • [5 pts] overall performance is shown with reasonable results (range, endurance, L/D, cruise speed and CL, CLmax, climb rate)
  • [5 pts] fully dimensioned aircraft with 3 views are shown.

Build:

  • [5 pts] evidence of an improved design through lessoned learned while building
  • [5 pts] evidence of good building technique
  • [5 pts] evidence of a built aircraft that (reasonably) matches the design

Fly:

  • [5 pts] evidence of good flight testing and observations from flight testing
  • [5 pts] evidence of improvements made from flight testing
  • [5 pts] evidence of good flight performance (videos or links to videos are welcome!)

Mission:

  • [5 pts] evidence of a reasonable computer strategy to direct pilot to waypoints.
  • [15 pts] reached all 3 waypoints (reasonably directly) while respecting boundary and ceiling limits. Plots shown and discussed.

Writing:

  • [5 pts] writing is clear.
  • [5 pts] report is well organized.
  • [5 pts] figures/data are nicely presented.
  • [5 pts] level of detail is appropriate.
  • [5 pts] good conclusions and reflections on the process

Extra Credit (levels, not cumulative):

  • [+5 pts] A proposed controller design
  • [+10 pts] Evidence of successful ground-based or simulation-based testing
  • [+40 pts] Successful mission with autopilot
