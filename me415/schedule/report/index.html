Project Report

due 12/13/2018 before midnight via Learning Suite 150 possible points

Format

Your report must be submitted in PDF format. The cover page should contain your team name, the names of all team members, and a nice photo (or rendering) of your aircraft. There is a page limit of 50 pages. Refer to the BYU ME Writing Materials for general writing guidance.

Rubric

Design (33%):

  • [5 pts] Overall configuration choices are appropriate and soundly justified.
  • [5 pts] Airfoil selection is appropriate and soundly justified.
  • [5 pts] Wing design is appropriate and soundly justified.
  • [5 pts] Predicted and measured weights are shown with discussion on how it affected the design if applicable. Design is not overly heavy.
  • [5 pts] Tail/canard/sweep sizing is appropriate and soundly justified.
  • [5 pts] A proper static and dynamic stability analysis is shown with discussion on how it affected the design.
  • [5 pts] An analysis of the propulsion system is included. System is efficient and suited to the aircraft.
  • [5 pts] Overall performance is shown with reasonable results (range, endurance, L/D, cruise speed and CL, CLmax, climb rate). Performance metrics are appropriate.
  • [5 pts] Fully dimensioned aircraft with 3 views are shown.
  • [5 pts] Overall quality of design analysis.
  • [+10 pts] (extra credit) Exceptional analysis and/or design novelty.

Build/Test (20%):

  • [5 pts] Evidence of an improved design through lessoned learned while building.
  • [5 pts] Evidence of good building technique.
  • [5 pts] Evidence of a built aircraft that (reasonably) matches the design.
  • [5 pts] Evidence of good flight testing and observations from flight testing.
  • [5 pts] Evidence of improvements made from flight testing.
  • [5 pts] Evidence of good flight performance (videos or links to videos are welcome!).
  • [+10 pts] (extra credit) Exceptional build techniques and/or innovation.

Mission (30%):

  • [10 pts] Completed mission. Respected boundary and ceiling limits. Trajectory plots shown and discussed.
  • [10 pts] Completed mission with 100 g payload (5 pts if 50 g payload).
  • [5 pts] Evidence of following good safety practices.
  • [5 pts] Good match/discussion in climb rate.
  • [5 pts] Good match/discussion in descent rate.
  • [5 pts] Good match/discussion in energy usage.
  • [5 pts] Overall mission quality.
  • [+5 pts] (extra credit) Highest L/D.
  • [+5 pts] (extra credit) Highest climb rate.
  • [+5 pts] (extra credit) Lowest energy usage.

Writing (17%):

  • [5 pts] Writing is clear and cohesive.
  • [5 pts] Report is well organized.
  • [5 pts] Figures/data are nicely presented.
  • [5 pts] Level of detail is appropriate.
  • [5 pts] Good conclusions and reflections on the process.
