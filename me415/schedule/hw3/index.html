HW 3: Wing Design

due 10/4/2017 before midnight via Learning Suite 50 possible points

  1. Design an appropriate wing for your project. Make sure the lift distribution provides a high span efficiency, and the cl distribution provides favorable stall characteristics. Provide the following:
    • A to-scale planform view of the wing.
    • A description and justification of how design choices were made.
    • The inviscid span efficiency at your cruise CL.
    • The wing CLmax (not the airfoil section clmax).
    • The approximate location of the wing aerodynamic center using the mean aerodynamic chord.

  2. Cut your wing using the foam cutter (group effort: one wing per project group). Take a picture of it and include it in the assignment. Describe any lessons learned or important details to reproduce.
