HW 4: Stability

due 10/11/2017 before midnight via Learning Suite 50 possible points

  1. Build a CAD model of your aircraft to estimate mass properties. Don’t forget to put in the elctronic components.
    • Report total mass (1), center of mass (3), moments of inertia (6)
    • Include a few views of your CAD model

    Note: I know you all know how to use CAD already so you can work together to create one model rather than having every team member create their own.

  2. Use XFLR5 or [AVL] to analyze the dynamic stability of your aircraft.
    • Plot the open loop eigenvalues and label each mode.
    • Report the static margin
    • Report all stability derivatives , which has a GUI and some helpful video tutorials here. To get started I’d look at videos 1, 2, 6, 7, 8.
  3. Design Review prep.
