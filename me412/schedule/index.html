  • Quizzes are on Learning Suite and are available after class on Fridays. They cover the material of that past week, and are due before class the following Friday.
  • Project guidelines are available here.
  • Deadlines for every assignment are also listed on Learning Suite.
  • Password-protected items Some handouts and solutions contain items that cannot be distributed beyond the class. Those are marked with a star, and will require authentication in order to download.
