Winglets: This is not just a tip effect, as discussed from a momentum point of view. It does alter the tip flow of course, but doesn't fundamentally change the generation of vortices, circulation, and induced drag. Winglets act as an effective span increase, but you know what else acts as an effective span increase? a span increase. Usually the main advantages of winglets are nonaerodynamic: lateral stability, gate or other span constraints, aesthetics and marketing.
