Schedule

  • Lecture slides are not full notes. Rather they are used to help provide organization, highlight some main points, and display items that would be time consuming to write down (e.g., images, long equations). Some students like to use the slides as a background to write their own notes on.
  • Homework assignments are listed on the day assigned. They are due one week later (Wed before midnight via Learning Suite).
  • Quizzes are on Learning Suite and are available after class on Fridays. They are due before class the following Friday.
  • In most cases, the lab writeup will be included in the week’s homework and should be turned in with the rest of the homework on Wednesdays. A full stand-alone labs (without an accompanying homework) are listed, and they will be due the following Wednesday as normal.
